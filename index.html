About Me

I am a developer from Los Angeles and have been working in the aerospace and defense industry for 3.5 years. My bachelor's degree in Cognitive Science led me to a curiosity in software development. As a logic puzzle enthusiast, I find code to be the most challenging but rewarding form of a puzzle. Finally piecing together the client and server-side of an application gives me joy. I love creative projects and find it exciting to see code come to life in useful applications.

When I'm not working on my technical skills, you can find me learning new things or planning out my next adventure (if I'm not already on one!). A couple of my favorite adventures include backpacking around Southeast Asia and camping through a cross-country road trip. Currently, I am trying to advance my artistry in sewing.
